About Us

OVERSEACE HITECH INDUSTRIAL COMPANY LTD is a distinguished Tanzanian logistics company based in Dar es Salaam, specializing in comprehensive clearing, forwarding, and logistics solutions. Since our establishment in 2013, we have strategically positioned ourselves as a key partner for businesses utilizing the Dar es Salaam Port.

Our strategic location enables us to serve not only Tanzania's 50+ million population but also the landlocked countries of Malawi, Zambia, Botswana, Zimbabwe, Democratic Republic of Congo, Burundi, Rwanda, and Uganda. We extend our services to customers using Julius Nyerere International Airport, providing seamless customs clearance for both import and export operations.

With over a decade of experience, we have built a reputation for reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction in the East African logistics landscape.

Our Vision

To modernize clearing, forwarding, and logistics services by offering products and services that are clearly exceptional in terms of innovation, quality, delivery, and value consistently across East Africa.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering uncompromising high-quality products and services with competitive pricing and timely delivery. Our goal is to achieve total customer satisfaction while maintaining the highest standards of integrity, trust, and respect in all our business relationships.

Our Journey

Company Foundation

December 3, 2013

OVERSEACE HITECH INDUSTRIAL COMPANY LIMITED was officially incorporated under Tanzania's Company Act No. 12 of 2002, marking the beginning of our journey in the logistics industry.

TRA Accreditation

2014

Achieved official accreditation from Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA) as a registered customs clearing and forwarding agent, establishing our credibility in the industry.

Regional Expansion

2016

Expanded operations to serve landlocked countries including Burundi, Rwanda, Uganda, and DRC, establishing ourselves as a key logistics partner in East Africa.

Fleet Modernization

2018

Invested in modern transportation fleet with GPS tracking systems, enhancing our capability to handle containerized, abnormal, and loose cargo efficiently.

Digital Transformation

2020

Implemented digital systems for cargo tracking, documentation management, and customer service, improving operational efficiency and transparency.

Decade of Excellence

2023

Celebrating 10 years of exceptional service with over 500 shipments cleared, 1000+ cargo transported, and 200+ satisfied clients across 8 East African countries.

Looking Forward

As we continue our journey, we remain committed to innovation, excellence, and expanding our services to meet the evolving needs of our clients across East Africa and beyond.
